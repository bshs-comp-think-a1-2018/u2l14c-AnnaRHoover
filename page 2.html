Picking For You

When picking a book for yourself you want to get want that interests you. If you are not interested in what you are reading, you won't want to read it.

Ask yourself first:

  • What genre do I like?
  • What age group am I in?
  • What are previous books I have liked before
  • What are authors that I have liked?

Unknown author, wikimedia commons, Public Domain

Asking and answering these questions will help you pick out an appropriate book for yourself. One that you will enjoy reading therefore will continue to read.
